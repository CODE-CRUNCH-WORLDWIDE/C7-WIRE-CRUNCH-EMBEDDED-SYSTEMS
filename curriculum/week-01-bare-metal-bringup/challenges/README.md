# Week 1 — Challenges

One challenge this week. It is harder than the exercises and lighter than the mini-project. Spend ~150 minutes on it. Skipping it is allowed for self-paced cohorts; you will regret it in Week 7 when the UART byte-loss bugs start.

1. **[Challenge 1 — UART hello-world](challenge-01-uart-hello-world.md)** — send `"crunch-wire week-01 boot ok\r\n"` from the Pi Pico W out UART0 at 115200 8N1, decode it on a USB-serial bridge to your laptop, and capture the byte timing on a logic analyzer.

Challenges differ from exercises in three ways: less hand-holding, an open-ended path, and a deliverable that lives at the *protocol* level (not the LED level). They are also where Week 1 stops being a tutorial and starts being engineering.
